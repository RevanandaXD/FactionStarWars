Pick Your Faction Nation

Galatic Republic

Guardians of peace and justice in the galaxy before the rise of the Empire. Led by the Galactic Senate and supported by the Jedi Order, the Republic stood for democracy, cooperation, and balance among the stars.

CIS

A separatist movement formed by star systems seeking independence from the Republic. Backed by powerful corporations and droid armies, the CIS fought for autonomy, control over their own resources, and liberation from centralized rule.

Galatic Empire

Formed from the ashes of the Republic by Emperor Palpatine, the Empire enforced order and control through fear, propaganda, and overwhelming military force. It valued unity, obedience, and galactic dominance above all else.

Rebel Alliance

A diverse coalition of freedom fighters who opposed the tyranny of the Empire. Though outnumbered and outgunned, the Rebellion stood for hope, liberty, and the dream of a free galaxy.
